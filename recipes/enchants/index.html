ENCHANTS

Many custom enchants has been added onto the server, and only more are going to come.
Despite having /cench info, multiple people still seem to not know what an enchant does, so here's a full list!

1. Types

There are 4 types of enchantments, each defining how an enchant should be categorised.

1.1. Regular

Regular enchants are the first type of enchants, and is a vanilla type. They are gray, and offer some advantage to the player. These enchants are:

  • Grindstonable
  • Kept on death
  • Disenchantable (T1) [WIP]

1.2. Curse

Curses enchants are the second type of enchants, and is a vanilla type. They are red, and hinder the player's progression by applying various debuffs. These enchants are:

  • Not grindstonable
  • Kept on death
  • Disenchantable (T4) [WIP]

1.3. Paradoxical

Paradoxical enchants are the third type of enchants, and is a new type. They are purple, and offer the player a bonus, but with a negative effect, similar to a monkey paw. These enchants are:

  • Not grindstonable
  • Kept on death
  • Disenchantable (T3) [WIP]

1.4. Ethereal

Ethereal enchants are the fourth type of enchants, and is a new type. They are yellow, and greatly help the players in various way. They are extremely useful and generally offer the player major boosts. These enchants are:

  • Grindstonable
  • Not kept on death
  • Not disenchantable [WIP]
  • Only one ethereal enchant per item can be added
